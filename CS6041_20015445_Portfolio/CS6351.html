Physical Computing

Human / Electronic Devices / Interaction

The prototype. Interactive Plant.

An Interactive plant is the plant who is able to speak their mind with their own charactor. This plant could interaction with you. They want your attention and love to have you around.

Goal of the work :

This project aim to provoke people to interact with electronic objects. By enabling people to have more engagement with their everyday life stuff (An indoor decorative plant), via an Arduino and other electronic devices. Extending an Interaction between people and their plant.

In this project, we have not built a plant monitoring system or an automatic watering machine which cuts off the relationship between the person and the plant. Rather, we are using the electronic devices to express plant's emotions and feelings, enhanced interaction between humans and potted plants.

How to accomplish it:

Through various studies, it is clear that people have now lost interaction with the plant, in order to establish more connections between the plant and the owner. We applied the Tamagotchi concept to create scenes of interaction between humans and plants, and developed characters for plants. With the help of microcontroller (Arduino) and other electronic sensors, interaction with plants is possible. Moreover, electronic output devices connected to the plant facilitate direct communication with the potted plant.

What I've learned :

  • Experiment with a microcontroller (Arduino) and various electronic devices to help people communicate with everyday objects in a more imaginative way.
  • Knowledge about building basic sensors, including touch sensors and moisture sensors.
  • Learning more coding techniques with C language.

Improvement Area :

  • Apply product design knowledge to create a better appearance of the potted plant station, such as placing the LCD screen on the potted plant, hiding the wires, and then placing the Arduino in the box.
  • Make the plant social with other plants, use MQTT to connect the plant to the cloud. Apply the IoT concept to the project.
  • Add voice assistant features and make the plant speak (Original Speech)
