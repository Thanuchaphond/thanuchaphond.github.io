Photo Editing

Camera settings / Photo composition / Post processing

Photography. Fallen to the new life.

The Story :

"This picture shows the maple leaf, which has fallen to the ground. Once something that had life, it no longer does. However, it will be a source of new life as it decomposes and provides nutrients for other organisms."

Goal of the work :

To show the ability to compose, setting the camera, take and edit photographs by using Photoshop. Create high-quality and meaningful photos with life as the theme.

What I've learned :

This is the first time I have learned composition techniques and understand the light that can bring different results to pictures when taking a new aspect (like this photo), simplicity may have a greater impact on the viewer. And I could make the ordinary things to be extraordinary. The photo editing tools and techniques I have learned can help me greatly enhance the attractiveness of my photos, and even polish my photos to make them more stunning.

Improvement Area :

I will apply lighting techniques on the left side of the picture to create dark colors and shadows on the upper right side of the picture to have a greater impact on the maple leaf. To creating a better picture, I would polish the dust from weeds at the bottom left of the picture, in this picture it still catches the viewer eyes on them, may be using the stamp tool in photoshop to help create consistently of the weeds in the background.
